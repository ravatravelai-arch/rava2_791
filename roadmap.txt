
RAHNAM PROJECT ROADMAP (FINAL MVP SPEC)
=======================================
REFERENCE: upgrade_plan.txt (V3.0)

PHASE 1: SMART TRAVEL WALLET (CORE UTILITY)
-------------------------------------------
Goal: Eliminate travel stress.
1. **The Safe Haven Card:**
   - Logic: Query `trips` table for `type='hotel'` and `date >= now`.
   - UI: Sticky header in "My Trip".
   - Action: One-tap "Take me to Hotel" (Native Language Address + TTS).
2. **Magic Scan (OCR):**
   - User uploads Ticket/Voucher image.
   - **Edge Function:** `process-ticket` (Gemini Vision + Zod Validation).
   - Stored in `trips` table.
3. **Timeline:**
   - Vertical timeline of events (Flight -> Hotel -> Tours).

PHASE 2: EXPLORE & VIBE FEED (GEOSPATIAL)
-----------------------------------------
Goal: Discovery based on "Mood", not just "Category".
1. **Spatial Search:**
   - **RPC:** `search_nearby_places` (PostGIS).
   - Filters: "Luxury" (Pol-dar), "Budget" (Moft-gardi), "Hidden Gems".
2. **AI Curator:**
   - "Daily Suggestion" box based on Weather + Time + Location.
3. **Visual Feed:**
   - Instagram-story style cards for nearby attractions (Cached Data).

PHASE 3: SURVIVAL TOOLKIT (TOOLS TAB)
-------------------------------------
Goal: The Swiss Army Knife.
1. **Bargain Calculator:**
   - Input: Lira/Dirham. Output: Toman (Real-time).
   - Feature: "Is it worth it?" button (AI valuation).
2. **Emergency Flashcards:**
   - Big text + Audio (e.g., "I have a nut allergy", "Where is the WC?").
3. **Metro Map:**
   - Offline interactive map.

PHASE 4: SOCIAL & GAMIFICATION (PASSPORT)
-----------------------------------------
Goal: Retention & Crowdsourcing.
1. **Digital Passport:**
   - Geo-fencing triggers: "You visited Burj Khalifa!".
   - Stamping the passport adds "Fuel" (Time).
2. **Footprints:**
   - Waze-style social notes on map (e.g., "Great coffee here").
   - AI Moderated (Text Classification).
3. **Price Watch:**
   - User reports price change -> Uploads photo -> AI verifies -> Earns Fuel.

PHASE 5: ARCHITECTURE & INFRASTRUCTURE
--------------------------------------
1. **PostGIS Migration:** Switch all location logic to `GEOGRAPHY(POINT, 4326)`.
2. **Edge Functions:** Move OCR and Price Verification to server-side.
3. **Cost Optimization:** Implement "Essentials First" & "3D Icon" strategy.
