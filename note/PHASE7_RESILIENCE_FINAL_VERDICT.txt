3. IDEMPOTENCY & RETRY LOGIC:
- Problem: Network jitters caused SyncManager to stall or duplicate.
- Fix: Added process locks and sequential processing. If an action fails, 
  the loop breaks to preserve order, and the SyncManager waits for the next online event.
