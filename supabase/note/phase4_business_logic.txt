
BUSINESS RULES: PHASE 4 (THE FUEL LOOP)
---------------------------------------

1. PASSPORT STAMPING (GEO-CHECKIN)
- Trigger: User expands a POI details card OR system detects stay > 15min.
- Reward: +50 XP, +0.1 AI Hours (6 Minutes Fuel).
- UI: Stamp animation with sound effect.

2. FOOTPRINTS (SOCIAL NOTES)
- Goal: Create an "Iranian-only" tips layer on top of Google Maps.
- Rule: Max 150 characters.
- Moderation: AI (Gemini Flash Lite) checks for "Political", "Offensive", or "Ads".
- Reward: +20 XP per footprint.

3. PRICE WATCH (CROWDSOURCING DATA)
- Logic: User submits new price + Photo.
- Verification Logic (AI Task): 
  "Is the number X visible on this menu/tag? If yes, verify."
- Reward: +0.5 AI Hours (30 Minutes Fuel).
- Purpose: Keeps our 'localPriceHint' updated without manual work.

4. WALLET DISPLAY
- Display in Minutes: `Math.floor(balance * 60)`.
- Icon: Fuel/Battery icon that fills up.
